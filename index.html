About Me

I am a physicist & chemist. I now work on R&D for 3D X-ray imaging devices, and previously I worked in molecular dynamics research. In particular, I have worked with Coulomb explosion imaging and time-resolved photoelectron spectroscopy.

I did my PhD, followed by a short postdoc, in Russell Minns' group at the University of Southampton (2017-2021), and then worked as a postdoc in Michael Burt's group at the University of Oxford (2021-2024).

Many of the contact methods associated with those positions will no longer reach me, so I have made this page to share my current contact information with former coworkers, collaborators, and anyone else who may find it useful.
